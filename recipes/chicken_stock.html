Chicken stock

Description

Chicken stock for soups or risottos.

Make use of your roast chicken left-overs by making a delicious stock!

Ingredients

  • Whole chicken carcus
  • Water ~2l
  • 3 onions cut roughly
  • Carrots cut in chuncks
  • Garlic
  • Celery
  • Herb + spring onion bunch

Steps

  1. Add everything into big pot
  2. Bring to the boil and turn down low
  3. simmer for at least 3 hours
  4. Skim off top layer as needed
  5. Add more water if necessary (it should make around 1 litre of stock at end)
  6. Strain and put in container to cool
  7. Refridgerate for at least 30 mins
  8. Skim off the top layer and you are good to go!
