Nachos

Description

This is a beanless nacho recipe with 'Riley-safe' ingredients!

The key is Beef mince, stay away from Pork mince or blends.

Ingredients

  • Beef mince
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Tomatoe puree
  • Corn & pea & carrot mix
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Nacho seasoning
  • Garlic salt
  • Grated cheese (vegan)
  • Corn chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220 degree celcius
  2. Chop onion and crush garlic, fry in coconut oil on medium/high heat until soft
  3. Add mince and fry until browned. Season well
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and tomato puree
  5. Add vegatables and simmer for 30 minutes
  6. Serve the cooked mince on a bed of nacho chips and top with grated cheese
  7. Bake in oven until cheese has melted and chips are crispy
  8. Enjoy! (Don't touch the plate! It's HOT!)
