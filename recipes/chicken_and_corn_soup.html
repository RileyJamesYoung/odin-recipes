Chicken and corn soup

Description

Put those chicken leftovers and homemade stock to the test with this delicious soup!

Best served with fresh toasted bread and butter

Ingredients

  • Corn (2 jars)
  • Chicken stock (1L)
  • Roast chicken (shredded)
  • Seasoning (veggie stock, salt)
  • Water (as needed)

Steps

  1. Wash and add 1 jar of corn to large pot
  2. crush corn to pulp (use masher, blender would be ideal)
  3. Add sencond jar of washed corn and stock to pot
  4. Heat to boil and then turn low to simmer
  5. Cook for 10 minutes then add shredded chicken
  6. Add water as needed and simmer for at least 10 more minutes (30-45mins is recommended)
  7. Toast that bread and butter it up
  8. Serve and enjoy! (Be careful! Its HOT!)
